Take Control of Your Finances with Budget Buddy!

|Smart Budgeting Made Simple--Track, Save, and Plan with Ease|

Once the end-version is launched, all will have access to the BFF Calculator but only Budget Bros will be able to make an account and reap the Budget Bros benefits.

The Benefits Include:

  • Easy Budget Tracking - Set and Monitor Budgets Effortlessly
  • Expense Insights - Understand Your Spending Patterns With Analytics
  • Multi Device Access - Open the Budget Buddy App on Both Mobile and Desktop
  • Hugs - Hugs is a Budget Bros feature that works on an interest due/interest paid system. Because Budget Buddy is an application to those serious about saving and budgeting, every Budget Bro that breaks their budget or pulls from savings will owe 10 cents for every dollar over budget/pulled from savings. This amount will be split up equally and paid to the platform’s Budget Bros at the end of every month in a name called Hugs.

  3. Track, Adjust, and Save - Get Insights, Stay on Track, Grow Savings!
