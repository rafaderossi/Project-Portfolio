Who is the Founder of Budget Buddy?

Rafael Phipps is an Argentine businessman that is known for his financial advising agencies located in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

He served in the U.S. Navy for 8 years and is now dedicated to helping individuals reach and sustain financial success. With over 10,000 5-star reviews, Mr. Phipps is known as one of the most credible financial advisors in Argentina.

His main hobby is football, and he supports his local team Club Atletico Platense who plays in Vicente Lopez in Argentina's top division of football. He enjoys reading fiction, and loves finding clever ways for people to make money.
